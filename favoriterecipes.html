Lasagna


Ingredients:

  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 (24 to 25-ounce) jar marinara sauce (3 cups), such as Rao's or Newman's Own
  • 12 ounces low-moisture mozzarella cheese, shredded (about 3 cups)
  • 15 dry lasagna noodles (not no-boil, about 2/3 of a 1-pound box)
  • 15 to 16 ounces whole-milk ricotta cheese (about 2 cups)

Instructions:

  1. Heat the oven to 400ÂºF.
  2. Brown the beef and onion.
    • Chop the onion
    • Add the onion, 1 pound lean ground beef, 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt, and 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper to a medium heat skillet
    • Cook until beef is cooked through (5-6 minutes)
    • Let sit for 5 minutes
  3. Spread a thin layer of the sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Stir the remaining sauce into the ground beef mixture.
    • Place 5 lasagna noodles in the baking dish, breaking them if needed to create a single layer
    • Spread 1 cup of the ricotta cheese over the noodles.
    • Cook until beef is cooked through (5-6 minutes)
    • Spread about 1 1/2 cups of the meat sauce on the ricotta, then sprinkle with 1 cup of the mozzarella.
  5. Repet Step 4 until the pan is baking dish is full
  6. Cover the dish tightly with aluminum foil and bake for 1 hour
  7. Let the lasagna cool for 15 minutes before serving
